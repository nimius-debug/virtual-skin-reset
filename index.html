Questions

Everything you want
to know first

Is this worth it without an in-person facial?

Yes — and often more so. Most skin frustration comes from using the wrong products in the wrong order, not from skipping facials. The right routine consistently applied at home creates more long-term change than a single treatment. Laura's training means every recommendation is backed by real clinical knowledge, not a quiz algorithm.

What do I need to prepare?

Complete the skin intake form and submit clear, makeup-free photos of your skin (front, side, close-up) plus photos of every product you currently use — at least 24 hours before your appointment. That's it.

What skin concerns can Laura help with?

Acne, melasma, post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH), barrier damage, dryness, dullness, uneven texture, and signs of aging. Laura also covers lifestyle, diet, stress, hormones, and any medications that may be affecting your skin.

How do the 3-month check-ins work?

Once a month for three months after your consultation, Laura reaches out via text to check in on your skin's progress. If something isn't working — a product irritates you, your skin shifts — she'll adjust your routine. You're not left alone after the call.

Do I have to buy from your store?

No. Laura recommends from her curated professional range first, but accessible alternatives are included for every budget. The $50 credit is there when you're ready to try professional-grade products — zero pressure.

When will I see real changes?

Most clients notice meaningful improvement within 2–4 weeks of consistently following their custom routine. Acne can shift faster, while melasma and PIH take longer — which is exactly why the 3-month check-in structure exists.
